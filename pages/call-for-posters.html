Posters

Authors are invited to submit proposals for poster presentations at STAG 2022. We welcome posters which present recent results, work in progress, new ideas, small-scale projects and experiments which may be of interest to the community, but which are still too speculative, incomplete, or whose scope is not wide enough to warrant a full paper. Posters provide a good opportunity for displaying and discussing ideas. We encourage submissions in all areas of Computer Graphics, Computer Vision, Visual Computing, Extended Reality and related disciplines.

Posters will be displayed during the conference, whereas a dedicated poster session will run in conjunction with the social event. Authors of accepted posters are expected to attend to their posters during the poster session.

Submission instructions

Posters must be written in English and submitted in PDF format via EasyChair. The submission should consist of a 2-page poster description formatted according to the Eurographics Computer Graphics Forum publication guidelines (A LaTeX template for submitters is available at this link), and an optional preliminary version of the poster. Submitted posters will undergo a double-blind reviewing process. We therefore invite submitters to carefully remove all personal data from their papers (e.g. authors, affiliations, etc.).
The authors of accepted posters will be asked to submit the final version of their 2-page paper, which will be included in the conference proceedings.
