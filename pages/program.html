Technical Program

the program is in CET (local time)


The STAG 2022 conference will take place on November 17-18 in Cagliari, Italy. We will have two keynote speakers, four full papers sessions, a poster session, and the thesis award ceremony. Speakers might be interested in downloading the Power Point STAG 2022 template.
